Overview: Rise and Shine!

We are developing a highly personalized website that will serve as a morning organizer and day-starter, called Rise and Shine.
Rise and Shine is a creative web application that prioritizes organization and productivity by combining multiple everyday morning routine activities,
such as to-do lists, Google Calendar integration, rising news stories, and suggested activities.
Think of Rise and Shine as your personal assistant to start your day off on the right foot. By encapsulating critical personalized information in one
centralized location, we provide the opportunity for users to keep their life organized and help kickstart their morning off in a fantastic mood.
As busy college students, we understand how critical it is to manage time and tasks, and thus, we want to create a cohesive personalized experience
where users can view their tasks, schedule, and suggested activities for the day. This takes away the stress of managing your schedule, as well as provides
unique and fun ideas on how users can spend their time, based on their interests. All in all, Rise and Shine will present users with a cohesive and organized
website that plans out their day, facilitating a productive and well-planned out day.
